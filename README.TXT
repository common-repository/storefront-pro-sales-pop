== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can I customize the pop up? =

Go to the customizer, click `Sales Pop`, here you will find all customizing settings :)

= I dont see the sales pop ever after clicking cross `x` ro close popup. Is it normal? =

Yeah, Sales pop will be removed from all pages if user closes.

= Can I show the sales pop on all the pages? It is showing up only on shop and product pages. =

Yeah, In `Customizer` > `Sales Pop`, Choose `Site wide` in `Show sales pop` field. This will show sales pop everywhere, except cart and checkout pages ;)
